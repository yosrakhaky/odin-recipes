Beef and Broccoli Rice Bowl

Description

This is my favourite base recipe for cream of mushrooms, perfect for soup season!

Ingredients

  • mushrooms (sliced)
  • broth
  • thyme
  • flour
  • butter
  • onions

Steps

  1. Heat butter in a pot
  2. Saute onions and garlic
  3. Add mushrooms
  4. Add flour to make roux
  5. Add broth
  6. Cover and allow to simmer
