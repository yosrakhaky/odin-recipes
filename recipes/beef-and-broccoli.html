Beef and Broccoli Rice Bowl

Description

I discovered this recipe when meal prepping back in my university days, and it's one of my favourites to this day!

Ingredients

  • rice
  • Beef (ground beef or cubes)
  • soy sauce
  • brown sugar
  • garlic
  • green onions
  • sesame
  • broccoli

Steps

  1. Cook rice
  2. Cook beef
  3. Whisk together soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, ginger in a small bowl
  4. Stir in broccoli, green onions and rice into ground beef
