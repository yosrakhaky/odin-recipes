Apple Crumble

Description

I've tried a few apple crumble recipes over the years and this one is my favourite so far!

Ingredients

  • apples
  • flour
  • sugar
  • oats
  • cinnamon powder
  • unsalted butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Peel apples and cut them into cubes
  3. Sprinkle toppings on apples in a bowl
